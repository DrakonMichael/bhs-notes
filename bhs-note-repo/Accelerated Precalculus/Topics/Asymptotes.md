### Vertical asymptote
The line $x=a$ is a vertical asymptote $iff \hspace{1.5mm} f(x)$ approaches $\pm\infty$ as $x$ approaches $a$.
Formally:
$$a \mid \lim_{x \to a^-} f(x) = \pm\infty \vee \lim_{x \to a^+} f(x) = \pm\infty$$ ^6e4133
