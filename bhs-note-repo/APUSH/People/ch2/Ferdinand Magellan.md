## Ferdinand Magellan
1480 (Sabrosa, Portugal) — 1521 – [[Chapter 2]]

---
Portuguese navigator who led the Spanish expedition of 1519-1522 that was the first to sail around the world, despite losing 3/5 ships to mutinies.
